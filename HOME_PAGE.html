Welcome to EcoSmart Waste Management Portal

EcoSmart Waste Portal helps individuals and communities learn how to manage waste responsibly through technology, education, and interactive tools. From identifying waste types to chatting with our Eco Bot, we're making waste disposal smarter, cleaner, and more efficient.

About

EcoSmart Waste Portal is an interactive platform designed to simplify and promote effective waste management practices. It leverages AI and digital tools to help users:

  • Understand and learn proper waste segregation
  • Predict waste type using image recognition
  • Identify the correct waste category instantly
  • Engage through quizzes to boost awareness
  • File complaints about waste issues
  • Get instant help via the Eco Bot chat assistant

Our goal is to make waste management easy, educational, and accessible to all.
